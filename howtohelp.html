Green Peace

Founded by Irving Stowe and Dorothy Stowe, along with a group of activists, in Vancouver, Canada, in 1971. The organization was initially established to protest nuclear testing in Alaska but has since become known for its environmental activism on various issues, including climate change, deforestation, and ocean pollution.

Sierra Club

The Sierra Club was founded by John Muir in 1892. John Muir was a Scottish-American naturalist, author, environmental philosopher, and early advocate for the preservation of wilderness in the United States. He founded the Sierra Club as a conservation organization dedicated to protecting the natural environment, particularly in the Sierra Nevada mountains of California.
